## ðŸ“Š Features

### 1. Business Problem & Goal
A retail chain struggled to identify which regions and product sub-categories were driving actual profit versus just top-line revenue. The goal of this dashboard is to provide a self-service interface for Store Managers and Regional Directors to:
* Track real-time KPIs (Sales, Profit, Quantity).
* Identify underperforming product lines.
* Optimize logistics by analyzing shipping mode distribution.

### 2. Visual Walkthrough
* **Executive Scorecard:** High-level KPI cards for Total Sales ($2.2M+), Total Profit, and Quantity to provide an immediate "health check" of the business.
* **Geographic Analysis:** A map visualization identifying sales penetration across different states and regions.
* **Category Decomposition:** Interactive bar and donut charts that allow users to drill down from broad Categories (e.g., Technology) into specific Sub-Categories (e.g., Copiers).
* **Logistics Breakdown:** Analysis of Shipping Modes (Economy, Priority, etc.) to understand customer fulfillment preferences.

### 3. Business Impact & Insights
* **Margin Optimization:** Identified that while certain categories have high sales volume, specific sub-categories (like Tables) were yielding lower profit margins, suggesting a need for inventory strategy shifts.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Pinpointed the "Central" region as a high-volume area, providing data to support localized marketing and supply chain allocation.
* **Customer Segmentation:** Isolated "Consumer" vs. "Corporate" performance, allowing for tailored promotional strategies based on segment profitability.
